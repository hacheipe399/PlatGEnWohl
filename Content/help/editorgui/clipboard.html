Editor GUI > Clipboard (Copy/Cut/Paste)

You can copy, cut and paste every block, bgo, npc and water-area in the level editor.

If you want to paste something your cursor switches to a paste icon and you enter paste-mode. In this mode you can paste your copied items. Items will be pasted from the top-left corner of your cursor.
